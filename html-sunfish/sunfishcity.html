Consider the sunfish, the most noble of all of god's creatures. Its favorite food is the delicious canteloupe gazpacho. It dislikes podcasts and likes paragliding. It is currently cleaning your kitchen counters, just to help out around the house. On Sundays, the noble sunfish makes lemonade.

Sunfish have strong opinions on the consistency of pancake batter. They have weak opinions on the consistency of cake batter. They have mediocre opinions on the consistency of cookie batter.

Sunfish Typography:

The Noble Ocean Sunfish

The noble ocean sunfish floats peacably through the ocean. It has a normal amount of parasites attached to it. The noble ocean sunfish loves to sunbathe and drink tea. We should all aspire to be like the noble ocean sunfish.

Elemental Affinities:

  • Water
  • Solar
  • Tea


The Noble Aquarium Sunfish

The noble aquarium sunfish floats peacably through the aquarium. It has an abnormal amount of parasites attached to it. The noble aquarium sunfish loves to moonbathe and drink coffee. We should all aspire to be like the noble aquarium sunfish.

Elemental Affinities:

  • Water
  • Lunar
  • Coffee
  • Customer Service


The Noble Ghost Sunfish

The noble ghost sunfish hunts humans through museums after the museum closes. It has no parasites attached to it. The noble ghost sunfish loves to impale its prey upon its spiny bones. We should all aspire to run away from the noble ghost sunfish.

Elemental Affinities:

  • Air
  • Ghost
  • Bloodlust
  • Customer Disservice

IF YOU ENCOUNTER A GHOST SUNFISH:

  1. Stop!
  2. Brandish your personal copy of Moby-Duck by esteemed author Donovan Hohn
  3. Pray to the nearest deity (geographic)
  4. Pray to the nearest deity (semantic)

REMEMBER: The ghost sunfish is very hungry, and it has not yet been taught mercy. The best way to survive a ghost sunfish is to not encounter one. Avoid areas such as natural science museums, large container ships mid-ocean, and your bedroom closet on nights of the full moon.
